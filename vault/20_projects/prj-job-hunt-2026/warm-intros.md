## Outreach Patterns

**Inside-contact message** (sent same day as application — per master plan Phase 5 Task 5.2 Step 4):

> Hey [name] — applying for [role] at [company]. Wanted to flag it in case there's any context worth me knowing or anyone on the team you'd suggest I connect with. Either way, no pressure — appreciate you.

**Warm-intro request** (when asking a strong-tie person to introduce you):

> Hey [name] — I'm targeting [target company] for an [role family] role. Saw you're connected to [target contact]. Would you be open to a quick intro, or would it be more useful if I reached out directly and mentioned you? Either works.

**Reactivation message** (for connections you haven't talked to in 6+ months):

> Hey [name] — been a minute. I left The Block last week and I'm running a focused job-hunt sprint targeting AI PM / Forward Deployed Product / Agent Ops roles. I've been building out [one specific artifact — MCP server / agent fleet / 4Q artifacts] and would love your read on whether [their company] might be a fit. Open to grab time, or happy to send the portfolio over for an async look.
