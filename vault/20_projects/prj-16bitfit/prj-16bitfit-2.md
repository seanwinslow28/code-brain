# 16bitFit

16bitFit
Using my own data and docs at first to create the voices, SFX, and the simple data that is required.
Creating a silly motivation gamified app that feels like a Sega or NES game. I want it to be similar to a tamagotchi, but more advanced. It’s in the style of Street Fighter. 
You create your 16 bit character and then it grows like a tamagotchi every time you give it something healthy, do a workout, or do any form of exercise. It dies a little and gets sick and overweight little by little when you give it something unhealthy or skip workouts. This is a way to track your eating habits and keeping you motivated to keep eating well. 

As you continue to feed it well and give it workouts (could be synced with your health app and Apple Watch) you level up. It can give you workout tips as well. The stronger you get, the more your characters stats improve. As you get stronger, you can use your character to fight the boss. If your character isn’t strong enough and have great stats, you’ll lose, so it’ll motivate you to improve every day so you can beat the boss. 

The better you feed it, the more you exercise, the better fighter it becomes. 

For later models, If I decide to incorporate AI, it can be that people send in a photo of themselves and the character that they use is 16 bit version of themselves in gym clothes. Another way to incorporate AI would to eventually have it track your calories and also prove that your meal is healthy with taking photos of what you’re eating. It would show the macro nutrients and micro nutrients. Telling you how it helped you in your health journey and why it’s good or bad for your body.
