## Synthesis

The tension between AI's speed and the human need for quality creates a dependency on supervision, which is enabled by intent engineering and validated by knowledge-lint. Intent engineering sets the boundaries and goals, supervision evaluates the output against those goals, and knowledge-lint ensures the underlying information is accurate. This loop creates a robust system for leveraging AI without sacrificing quality or accuracy. The consequence is that humans can scale their impact by automating the generation of content while retaining control over its quality and relevance.
