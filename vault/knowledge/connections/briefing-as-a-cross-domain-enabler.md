## Synthesis

The shift from prompting to briefing enables consistent, high-quality AI interactions across domains like creative production, knowledge synthesis, and job preparation. The mechanism for briefing ensures that whether Sean is working on creative scripts, refining knowledge vaults, or preparing for job interviews, the same structured intent can be communicated to AI agents. This enables a unified workflow across domains and reduces the need for domain-specific prompting techniques.
