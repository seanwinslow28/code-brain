## Synthesis

A tension exists between the autonomy of agents in knowledge management workflows and the need for strict constraints to maintain data integrity. Infrastructure Status acts as a control layer enforcing boundaries, while Agent Health Monitoring tracks whether agents uphold those constraints. If an agent fails within these limits, it risks exposing sensitive data like interview notes or referrals, which could jeopardize Sean's job-hunting and personal branding efforts.

## Implications

- Failure to enforce constraints may lead to unauthorized modifications of sensitive data in Sean's personal knowledge vault.
- Without consistent health monitoring, agents may operate outside of defined boundaries without detection, risking both data integrity and security.
