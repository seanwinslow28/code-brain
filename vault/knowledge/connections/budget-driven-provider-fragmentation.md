## Synthesis

The tension between cost-capped automation and the need for comprehensive research forces a fragmentation of the provider landscape. When the primary automated agent hits its budget cap, the workflow does not stop but instead fractures into multiple manual interventions across different vendors. This creates a hidden dependency where the quality of the final knowledge artifact depends not just on the research depth, but on Sean's ability to manually orchestrate and synthesize outputs from disparate, non-integrated tools.
