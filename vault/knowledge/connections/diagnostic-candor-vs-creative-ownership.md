## Synthesis

The tension lies between the requirement for absolute candor in feedback and the necessity of preserving the creator's ownership of the solution. The Brain Trust model enforces a diagnostic-only role where peers identify structural fractures without dictating repairs, creating a dependency on the 'plussing' mechanic to maintain upward iteration. This prevents defensive entrenchment but requires the creator to possess the internal capacity to translate abstract structural diagnoses into concrete narrative repairs without external prescription.
