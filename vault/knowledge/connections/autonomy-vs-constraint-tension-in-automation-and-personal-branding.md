## Synthesis

There exists a tension between the autonomy of agents in knowledge management workflows and the need for strict constraints to uphold data integrity and personal branding. While automation relies on agents operating independently, System Constraints enforce boundaries that prevent unauthorized modifications of sensitive data such as interview notes or referrals. This creates a friction point where automation’s efficiency is balanced against the need for accountability, as failure to uphold constraints can lead to breaches that impact Sean's professional efforts.
