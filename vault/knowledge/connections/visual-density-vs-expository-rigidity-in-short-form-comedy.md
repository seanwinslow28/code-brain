## Synthesis

The tension between Miyazaki's ekonte methodology and traditional Western screenplay formats reveals a critical constraint for Sean's 3-5 minute animated shorts: standard script formats fail to capture the required density of an animated frame because they separate writing from visual composition. By adopting the ekonte, Sean can force the narrative to develop organically alongside spatial geography and kinetic timing, which is essential for maintaining comedic pacing in a compressed format. This shift transforms the screenplay from a static text document into a dynamic technical blueprint that dictates precise mechanical instructions for the animation pipeline.
