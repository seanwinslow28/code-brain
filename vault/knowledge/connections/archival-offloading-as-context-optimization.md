## Synthesis

The tension between comprehensive record-keeping and efficient AI processing is resolved by decoupling the active roadmap from its historical log. This pattern reveals that maintaining a 'living' document requires active pruning of completed work to preserve signal-to-noise ratio for agents. The consequence is a more robust agentic workflow where the cost of context is minimized, and the relevance of strategic inputs is maximized by excluding finished tasks.
