## Synthesis

The interplay between Automation Routines and the Accountability Gap reveals a tension where automation's efficiency gains inadvertently undermine human motivation. This pattern creates an invisible but critical dependency: while automation tracks agent health, it does not replace the need for structured feedback that aligns human performance with organizational goals. The consequence is increased attrition and disengagement among high-potential professionals like Sean, who may seek external validation or new environments when internal feedback systems fail.
