## Synthesis

There is a fundamental tension between the high-density, low-latency comedy mechanics of Tina Fey and the surreal, emotional depth of Donald Glover. Fey's system relies on 'register collision' and 'joke layering' to maximize comedic payload per minute, creating a machine-gun effect. Glover's system relies on 'surrealist poetry' and emotional subversion, where the comedy emerges from the gap between expectation and reality. For Sean, this tension manifests in the 'Screenplay Craft Architect' role: he must decide whether to prioritize the measurable density of Fey's writing room methodology or the emotional resonance of Glover's auteur approach, as these two systems often pull against each other in short-form animation.
