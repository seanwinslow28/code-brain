## Definition

A type of technical interview where candidates are expected to code in real-time while explaining their thought process, often with an emphasis on problem-solving and communication.

## Context

This format is increasingly common in tech interviews, especially for roles involving product management and engineering.

## Examples

- Plan-Build-Review structure at Sierra
- 45-minute live build at Google AI PM
- Take-home with deployed prototype at Stripe and Netflix
