## Definition

A research framework designed to handle complex, multi-target queries that LDR cannot manage due to timeouts or citation quality issues.

## Context

Gemini DR is utilized for compound topics, due-diligence matrices, or evaluations spanning multiple dimensions.

## Examples

- Comparing A/B/C on metrics 1–4 for product evaluation.
- Analyzing citation quality across three academic domains.
