## Definition

A snapshot of the operational status and health of a group of autonomous agents, including their last run times, statuses, resource usage, and any notes about their execution.

## Context

Fleet status provides insight into the overall health of automated systems and helps identify failures or bottlenecks. This is crucial for maintaining reliable knowledge management workflows.

## Examples

- vault-indexer (2:00 AM daily, Mac Mini, $0.00/run) - healthy
- daily-driver morning (8:45 AM daily, Claude API, ~$0.40/run) - healthy but no daily note generated
