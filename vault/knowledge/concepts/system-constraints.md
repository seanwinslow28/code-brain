## Definition

System Constraints refer to the limitations placed on workflows and processes within a system, such as time limits or resource availability. These constraints drive the need for efficient task allocation and prioritization.

## Context

Understanding system constraints is critical for optimizing performance, ensuring timely delivery of results, and managing complex workflows effectively.

## Examples

- Hard time limit (900 seconds) for LDR
- Splitting complex queries into Gemini DR
