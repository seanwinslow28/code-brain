## Definition

The excessive use of AI tokens due to inefficient habits, which significantly increases the cost of AI usage without providing proportional value. This is often a result of unoptimized prompts and workflows.

## Context

For Sean, understanding token waste is critical in managing costs associated with AI tools like Claude. Efficient token usage aligns with his broader goal of optimizing workflows in both creative and professional contexts.

## Evidence

> Frontier AI running on the most expensive models available costs pennies when you know what you’re doing.

> They’re burning 5x, 10x, sometimes 20x what they should on the exact same work, and they think that’s just what AI costs.

## Examples

- Users spending more than a quarter on Claude for simple tasks, while production pipelines do the same work for less.
- Sessions with poor prompt structure leading to inefficient token consumption.
