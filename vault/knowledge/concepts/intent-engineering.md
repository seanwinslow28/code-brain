## Definition

A discipline focused on defining structured intent specifications for AI agents, encompassing boundaries, success criteria, and decision rules. It ensures an AI agent does the exact thing intended by its creator rather than a similar but divergent action. This discipline is particularly valuable in agentic systems where precise alignment between human intent and agent behavior is critical.

## Context

For Sean, intent engineering is essential in aligning his AI agents with the precise goals of his job-hunting strategy and personal knowledge vault. The clarity it provides ensures that AI tools do not deviate into tangential tasks, which is crucial when managing high-stakes outcomes like career transitions.
