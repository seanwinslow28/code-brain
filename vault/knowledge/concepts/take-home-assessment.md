## Definition

A form of interview where candidates are given a coding or design task to be completed outside the immediate interview setting.

## Context

This format is used by companies like Stripe, Netflix, and Scale AI to assess a candidate's ability to work independently.

## Examples

- Multi-day take-home with deployed prototype at Stripe and Netflix
- 2-hour assessment likely at Glean
