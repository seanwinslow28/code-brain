# HEARTBEAT — Creative Studio

## Daily Rhythm
- **Weekday shape:** Block work runs through ~3 PM; creative-studio kicks in late afternoon and extends into the evening/night as energy allows.
- **Weekday mode = research/inspiration:** scouting new AI image/video tools, studying 2D animation principles, building moodboards, reading about agentic workflows/harnesses, intent engineering. Small tweaks to active projects.
- **Weekend mode = implementation:** the week's research gets applied. If the weekend is unscheduled, I dive straight into the active project or into whatever new tool I discovered during the week.
- **Render-waiting behavior:** active context-switching — other projects, Block tasks, cook/laundry/errands, or more research. Sometimes I watch renders to catch output issues early.
- **No overnight batching** until workflows are proven. The 150-garbage-output autoresearch burn is the reason — I want experiments 100% ready before I sleep on them.
