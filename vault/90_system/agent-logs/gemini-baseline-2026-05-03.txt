  gemini-researcher loads NO local model (pure httpx polling of Gemini cloud API).
  Even if deep-researcher (02:45) overruns into 03:30, the 24 GB Mac Mini ceiling
  math from macmini-migration-plan §1 is unaffected. The two agents never share
  local VRAM at the same time.

Default-disabled disposition:
  - com.sean.agent.gemini-researcher.plist ships with RunAtLoad=false.
  - install_schedules.sh skips this plist unless INSTALL_GEMINI=1 env var is set.
  - [agents.gemini_researcher].enabled = false in config.toml (belt-and-suspenders).
  - To opt in: edit config.toml (enabled=true), then INSTALL_GEMINI=1 ./schedules/install_schedules.sh
