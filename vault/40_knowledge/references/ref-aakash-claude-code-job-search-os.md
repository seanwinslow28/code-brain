## What Your Morning Looks Like

30-minute one-time setup. Then:

- **You open your laptop. Your daily briefing is waiting.** The OS scanned your target companies for new postings in the last 24 hours. The top roles that match your profile are scored and ranked. A *customized resume for each one*, built from your real experience. Already reviewed and corrected. Ready to submit.
- **Below the roles: your outreach for the day.** Draft [messages](https://www.news.aakashg.com/p/writing-cold-emails-to-get-referrals) personalized to each person. Follow-ups for recent connections. [Referral](https://www.news.aakashg.com/p/referrals-to-get-jobs) requests for roles you applied to. Nudges for referrals that have gone quiet.
- **Prep for your upcoming interviews.** The questions that company actually asks (from web research plus insider data from hundreds of candidate debriefs), the interviewer’s background, recent product news, and a game plan for your specific candidacy gaps. [Mock interviews](https://www.news.aakashg.com/p/conquer-behavioral-interviews-forever) grade your answers and tell you which stories to swap in.
- **After the interview.** Every question categorized. Every answer scored. The exact moments where you left value on the table, with rewrites. A personalized thank-you email referencing what you *actually discussed*. Each interview makes the next one sharper.
- **Your daily time: 20-30 minutes.** The system handles the rest. The OS can apply to as many roles as match your criteria. The difference is that *every single application* is tailored, reviewed, and backed by a referral strategy. Volume AND precision.

If you’re full-time job searching after a layoff, this is a *no-brainer*. The OS does in 20 minutes what used to take 3 hours. The math on ROI is simple: the average job switch leads to a 10-15% raise. At a $200K salary, that’s $20-30K. This costs $49.
