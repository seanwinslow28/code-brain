### Google Core Update Results (6 days post-rollout)

- Overall positive performance across metrics
	- Total views increased 16%
	- Sessions and new users increased significantly
	- Organic new users and engagement rate up substantially
- Search Console improvements
	- Clicks increased ~50%
	- CTR improved slightly
	- Impressions decreased 10% (expected due to Google’s inflated impression fix)
- Google Discover performance strong
	- Clicks increased 26%
	- Impressions up despite overall impression deflation
- Google News exceptional growth
	- Clicks increased 67%
	- Impressions up 50%
