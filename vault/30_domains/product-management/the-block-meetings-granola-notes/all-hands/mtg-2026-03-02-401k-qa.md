# 401k Q&A

### 401k Plan Transition Overview

- Moving from Justworks multi-employer plan (Empower) to Fidelity single-employer plan
- Required due to leaving Justworks platform
- Benefits: Can keep Fidelity plan even if switching HR providers later
- Launch: March 2026 with manual data management initially, then automated

### Contribution Details & Matching

- Deadline: Make deferral elections by March 5
- Company matching: 4% of eligible compensation
	- Includes base salary, bonus, and commission
	- Per pay period matching only (no true-up)
	- If max out contributions early, no additional matching later in year
- Auto-enrollment: 3% default contribution
	- Must actively change to 0% if no contribution desired

### Account Management & Transfers

- All Empower balances will be liquidated and wired to Fidelity
- Blackout period during transition
- Self-directed brokerage accounts available
	- Fill out SDB form online or call Fidelity
	- Must wait until blackout period ends
- Brokerage portions from Empower will be liquidated like standard transfers

### Safe Harbor Status & Compliance

- Current plan (2026): Not safe harbor due to Jan-Feb gap
- 2027 plan: Will become safe harbor to avoid compliance testing
- Non-safe harbor risks:
	- Subject to discrimination testing (highly vs non-highly compensated employees)
	- Failed tests require refunding excess contributions
	- Pretax refunds become taxable
