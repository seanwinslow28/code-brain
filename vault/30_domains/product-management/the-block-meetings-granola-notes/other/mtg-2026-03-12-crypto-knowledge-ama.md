### Bitcoin Investment Philosophy

- Bitcoin maximalists generally insecure about other cryptocurrencies
	- Historical skepticism since 2016-2017 (predicted Ethereum failure)
	- View most altcoins as extraction schemes targeting retail
- Long-term investment framework: “coma test”
	- Which assets comfortable holding for 10-20 years?
	- Bitcoin has highest certainty due to established monetary value
	- First-mover advantage creates difficult-to-disrupt position
- Ethereum faces ongoing innovation pressure
	- Must constantly evolve to remain relevant
	- Solana example shows established chains can be surpassed
	- Dominates “safe DeFi” while Solana captures retail usage

### AI Agents and Protocol Security

- Current AI capabilities overestimated in crypto
	- Not sophisticated enough for autonomous exploitation yet
	- Guided by security researchers, models finding serious vulnerabilities
- Future threat timeline: multiple years before autonomous capability
	- Once capable, exploitation will happen quickly across all protocols
	- Bounty incentives significant (5-10% of protocol value)
	- Example: $100M protocol hack = $10M bounty
- Protocol hardening expected
	- AI will constantly test for gaps
	- Established protocols like Uniswap (7-8 years unhacked) relatively secure
	- New model breakthroughs will trigger rapid attack cycles
