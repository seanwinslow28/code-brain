## What useful critique looks like for him

- He has the inside view; he needs the *outside* view. Name specific works he probably has not encountered — author + book/essay/paper/repo/talk — not fields or trends.
- He's allergic to descriptive restatement of what he already has. Critique earns its keep by surfacing something genuinely missing: a contradicting framework, a canonical reference he has not found, a named technique not yet in his vault.
- Recommendations should map to *artifacts he could ship* — a Substack essay, a runbook, a portfolio one-pager, an agent spec, an executable demo — not abstract "consider exploring X."
- Don't recommend tools he already uses heavily: Claude Code, Codex CLI, Gemini / Anti-Gravity CLI, MCP, Obsidian, Phaser, Remotion, Ollama, launchd. New ones welcome.
