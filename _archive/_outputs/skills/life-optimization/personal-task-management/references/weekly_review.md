# Weekly Review Template

Use this template to conduct a structured Weekly Review. This ritual ensures nothing slips through the cracks and aligns daily work with long-term goals.

## Phase 1: Clear the Decks (Get Current)

**Goal:** Empty all capture points so your mind is free.

1.  **Loose Papers/Mail:** Scan or toss.
2.  **Digital Downloads:** Run `/organize-inbox` or clear `~/Downloads`.
3.  **Browser Tabs:** Bookmark or close. If it's a task, add to specific project.
4.  **Desktop:** Move files to their permanent home.

## Phase 2: Look Back (Review Past 7 Days)

**Goal:** Close open loops and celebrate wins.

1.  **Calendar Audit:**
    - Look at the past week's calendar.
    - Did you complete all meetings? Did any generate new tasks?
    - *Action:* Capture follow-ups.
2.  **Task Log Review:**
    - Review completed tasks. (Give yourself credit!)
    - Review uncompleted tasks. Only 3 options:
        1.  **Rollover:** Mark for next week.
        2.  **Delegate:** Assign to someone else.
        3.  **Delete:** If it wasn't important enough to do, maybe it never will be.

## Phase 3: Look Ahead (Plan Next 7 Days)

**Goal:** Anticipate collisions.

1.  **Calendar Scan:**
    - Look at the upcoming week.
    - *Risk Check:* Are there days with >4 hours of meetings? Block "Recovery Time".
    - *Prep Check:* Do any meetings require prep work? Schedule distinct tasks for them *now*.
2.  **Goal Alignment:**
    - Read your Quarterly Goals.
    - Ask: "What implies progress on these goals this week?"
    - Schedule at least one "Deep Work" block for a P0 goal.

## Phase 4: Get Creative (Someday/Maybe)

**Goal:** Dream a little.

- Review your "On Hold" or "Someday" list.
- Is there any project you want to reactivate?
- Any new crazy ideas? Capture them now.

## Output: The Weekly Plan

Generate a markdown file `Reviews/Weekly_[Date].md`:

```markdown
# Weekly Plan: [Date Range]

## 🏆 Top 3 Outcomes (Must Win)
1.
2.
3.

## 📅 Schedule at a Glance
- **Monday:** Admin + [Task]
- **Tuesday:** Deep Work on [Project]
- **Wednesday:** Meetings
...

## ⭕ Open Loops to Close
- [ ] Task 1
- [ ] Task 2
```
