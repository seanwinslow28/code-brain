**Gym Routine**

1. What's your split? (Push/pull/legs, upper/lower, bro split, full body, etc.)  
   1. I do the push, pull, legs split. Monday \- Push, Tuesday \- Pull (arm focus), Wednesday \- Legs/Abs, Thursday \- Push, Friday \- Pull(back focus). Then Saturday is usually walking on the treadmill/abs and Sunday is just walking on the treadmill or being outdoors (when it’s nice).    
2. What does a typical workout look like? (e.g., "Monday \= chest/tris: bench 4x8, incline DB 3x10..." — even rough is fine)  
   1. Pretty much explained it above. I don’t count reps because I just go to failure. I try to do 3-4 sets per exercise and I switch it up between free weights, calisthenics, machines, and smith machines to keep it interesting.  
3. Do you follow a program or go by feel?  
   1. I go by feel. I’ve watched enough youtube videos/worked out long enough to know what I like and what I don’t like. What I like is being able to go to the gym whenever I want and not have someone telling me what to focus on that day. I like being on my own schedule and not have someone breathing down my neck. I mentally trained myself to provide inspiration/motivation. Not an outside source.
